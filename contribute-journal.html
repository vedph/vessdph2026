Contribute to the journalprepare a package

Prepare a contribution to the Journal. Everything happens in your browser: your photo is resized and cleaned of metadata locally, and you get a small package to send to the organisers. Nothing is uploaded or published from this page.

Journal contributions are reviewed before publication. Please submit only material that you are willing to share with the Summer School community and, if approved, on the public website. If a photo shows recognizable people, please make sure they have agreed to publication. You may choose to be credited by name, by role, or anonymously.

Published contributions can be amended or removed on request by contacting the organisers.

All types are welcome, and a photo is optional for every one of them.
