**Venetian History, Urban Spaces and New Technologies**

The history of Venice and its urban space, seen through a Digital and Public Humanities lens. How can new technologies help us understand the Venetian past, and how can a public history approach engage audiences in research and narrative? GIS and mapping connect present-day locations to past life. The day mixes outdoor and indoor activities, with time to apply generative AI and virtual exhibitions to the history of Venice.
